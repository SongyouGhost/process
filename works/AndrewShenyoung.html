設計概念
Concept

以「時光膠囊」為出發點，在療癒的氛圍下打造少年感的青澀。
在成長的過程，期待自己是個好的大人，回應著過去的自我，又盼望著大人的自己。
似老舊的信封拿出，看著過去自己寫下信，並想告訴未來，現在看到的風景，撕裂的紙，放在過去的時光，堆疊而成的記憶，在數年之後從箱子中被拿出來。


Using "Time Capsule" as the starting point, the film creates a teenage sense of youth in a healing atmosphere.
In the process of growing up, I expect myself to be a good adult, responding to my past self and looking forward to my adult self.
The old envelope is taken out, and the letters that I wrote in the past and wanted to tell the future about the scenery I see now, the torn paper, the time in the past, and the memories that are piled up, are taken out of the box after several years.

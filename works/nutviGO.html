About

「nutviGO」是一個虛構品牌，我試著請 AI 扮演客戶，將接案的整個流程跑過一遍，讓 AI 提出需求，並由我完成，其中AI扮演一位新創營養師，試圖開發一款營養能量棒。

I tried to ask the AI to play the role of a customer, run through the whole process of taking a case, let the AI put forward the requirements, and I completed it, in which the AI played the role of a new nutritionist, trying to develop a product that replenishes energy at any time.

設計概念
Concept

以「極度濃縮營養」為核心發展，再稍微加入「自然」的意象，創造出專為健身的人士、忙碌上班族打造的潮流產品。

With the core development of "Extreme Nutrient Concentrate" and a little bit of "nature" imagery, we created a trendy product for fitness enthusiasts and busy office workers.

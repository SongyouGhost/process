漂幽部：不定時的設計、藝術書籍電子報
自己平常很常聽、看說書相關的頻道，但和設計與藝術相關的其實在市場上比較少，加上我也很常看相關的書，不如自己開始經營電子報吧!

Design and art book e-newsletter
I usually listen to and read book-related channels, but there are fewer design and art-related books in the market, and I often read related books, so why don't I start my own e-newsletter?

設計概念
Concept

「現在讀書才是次文化!｣
這是我在某個說書的 Podcast 聽到的，實際上現在會讀書的人越來越少了，比起以往會被稱為次文化的動漫畫、潮流等，讀書在現在才是次文化吧！所以試著把風格做的很次文化了。
字體設計以「深入」、「轉譯」、「滑順」作為電子報特色的表達，還是加回一些手寫感和明體的人文感，還是避免離說書的主題太遠。人物的部分以漂流在海上的感覺繪製。

Reading is now a subculture!
This is what I heard on a book podcast. In fact, fewer and fewer people can read nowadays, and reading is more of a subculture now than in the past, when it was called subculture like anime and manga, or trends, etc. So I try to make my style very subcultural! That's why I tried to make the style very subcultural.
The font design uses "in-depth", "translation", and "smooth" as the expression of the e-newsletter's features, but it still adds back some handwriting and a humanistic sense of the explicit style, and avoids straying too far from the theme of the book. The character's part is drawn with the feeling of drifting on the sea.

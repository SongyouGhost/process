在學校系服比賽的投稿，後來在投票中獲選，販售並製作給系上同學。

After submitting the design to the school uniform competition, the project was selected in the voting and was printed in large quantities for the students in the department.

設計概念
Concept

以字開始，試著以幾何，並且圓圓胖胖的台南感，畢竟大多學生來到台南第一件事是聯想到小吃，因此將關鍵字定調為：「可愛、茫茫的、台南、日式復古」，即便字是主要的，也增加了VCD的元素。
Starting with words, we tried to use geometry and a sense of round and chubby Tainan. After all, the first thing most students think of when they come to Tainan is snacks, so we set the keywords as "cute, blank, Tainan, Japanese retro", even though the words are the main ones, they also add the elements of the VCD.
